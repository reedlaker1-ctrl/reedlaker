Policies & Expectations

Zero Refund Policy

Because each ring is custom-made and manufactured direct-to-order, I maintain a zero-refund policy once production has begun.

No Resizing

Many of the materials used (especially alternative metals) cannot be resized. It is critical to confirm your size with a physical sizer before placing an order.

Timeline

Every piece is handcrafted to order. Most custom rings have an expected production timeline of 3–6 weeks.

Returns for Defects

Returns are accepted only if the product has a manufacturing defect or if the final product does not match the specifications of your original order.
